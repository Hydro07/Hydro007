Nepal – Heaven on Earth?

Being a Nepali I will agree that Nepal is very beautiful and can be compared with heaven on earth. The fact that me being a Nepali won’t make Nepal- heaven on earth. Diversification in geography makes Nepal different. Nepal is home to tall mountains in the Himalayas, rocky hills in the mid, and grassland like an African safari. Despite being a small nation, it has diversified land. So, there is a saying in Nepal that ” Heaven is a myth, Nepal is real “.
